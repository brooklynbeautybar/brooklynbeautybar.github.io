Brisbane Brows

Sculpting (Waxing)

Your brow sculpt starts off with a one on one, informative brow consult determining what shape will complement your unique features and frame your face. Followed by a detailed and pain-free experience to ensure your brow sculpt is perfectly tailored to you. Let us create the ideal shape for you at Brisbane's best brow salon.

Sculpting (Threading)

The “New” but “Old” technique of hair removal. Brow threading is a more gentle form of hair removal for your skin. As more and more people are using AHA’s in their skin care resulting in the area around the brow becoming quite sensitive to waxing, threading is a great alternative. With the added benefits of accuracy, no chemicals and being longer lasting you’ll be wishing you switched earlier.

Tinting

Whether your brows are light to medium in colour or contain those “naughty blondes”, tinting works for everyone. At its core, tint is applied to your brows to accentuate the shape and create a more fuller look. Tinting can also be highly effective in bringing out the colour of your eyes as well as complementing your hair and skin tones. Our experts can analyse your features for your perfect brow colour.

Lightening

We lighten your hairs using a high grade peroxide with a cooling blonde colour, which has the strength to lift the charcoal pigment gently from the hair while lightening at the same time. Most suitable for women with warm tones to the skin wanting to go lighter in hair colour.

Brow Extensions

Extensions are a fabulous way of filling in gaps to create a fuller canvas to your brows. Available in an assorted range of colours, each synthetic hair is applied to an existing hair or onto the skin to create the illusion of symmetry.

Brisbane Lashes Specialists

Classic Lashes Extensions

Whether your looking for a little more of an opened-eyed effect or just fuller lashes, Lash Extensions can give you the look you are after. With a single synthetic lash attached to your nature lash, this is the perfect treatment for someone wanting to look natural but amazing.

Lash Lifting

A modern version of the classic lash perm. Instead of curling lashes back, the Lash Lift will elevate your lashes upwards making your lashes look longer. Your eyes will look bigger, more open and lifted. This is a very popular option for anyone wanting low maintenance lash enhancing and can last up to 6 weeks.

Russian Lash Extensions

This set is for those who want to be noticed. The Russians were the first to invent the technique called 3D+ Russian Volume. They transform your sparse lashes into a luscious dramatic fullness that other extensions are not able to achieve. This method of applying 3 or more extremely thin, soft, light and high quality fan extensions onto one natural lash is unique and will ensure that your natural lashes stay healthy as well as dramatic and full volumed.

Lash Tinting

If you hate fussing with mascara every day, then you no longer need to, tint those luscious lashes instead. Using a safe specially formulated dye, your lashes can be tinted black or dark brown. Great for swimmers and holidaying.

Brisbane Eyebrow Tattooing

Feather Touch

Feather Touch is a drawn, hair stroke technique that is applied to create an illusion of naturally shaped brow hairs. Recommended to clients wanting a natural, stroked-hair look.

Block

Block is a deeper more defined colouring, a look that is completely filled in to create a defined backdrop for people that have light or few brow hairs.

Shading

Shading is an ombré technique that is perfect for people wanting a subtle background shade behind their brow hairs. Shading can be used as the perfect back drop for brows suited to the coloured-in look or need of depth of colour.

Shade & Blade

Shading with Feather Touch is used for a 2-3 dimensional look for people that like a shaded block as the perfect back drop, as well as wanting hair strokes over the top for a more natural wispy finish.
