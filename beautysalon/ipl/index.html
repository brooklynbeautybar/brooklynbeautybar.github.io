Super Hair Removal

Our latest technology of SHR brings speed and excellent comfort to treatments, achievable for IPL. This technology makes full body hair reduction fast and effective. One of the unique features of SHR is the opportunity to treat even dark skin types, which historically have been very problematic. This procedure is fast and produces amazing results.

Skin Rejuvenation

This amazing treatment is a combination of a collagen cream facial with IPL Skin rejuvenation technology. Created to reduce wrinkles and fine lines, brightening dull skin, making pores thinner and ideal for thick skin texture. Collagen enhanced skin will get its tonus back to significantly improving its cutaneous relaxation. Wrinkles and aging lines will be lifted, skin texture will become thinner giving a brighter face tone. The skin will be rejuvenated, repair with a younger aspect. The results will be visible from the first month and will progressively improve.

Pigmentation Removal

We use state of the art technology to treat melanin deposits and a wide range of pigmentation on most skin types. As the photons from the light hit the molecules of the pigmented area within the skin the light turns to heat, destroying the lesions, all while leaving the surrounding skin structure unaffected. The initial sensation is much like sunburn and will peel off like a scab to reveal a reduction of unwanted spots.
